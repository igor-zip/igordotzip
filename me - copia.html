Hi, I’m Igor. Nice to meet you.

This space was supposed to be my portfolio, but honestly, I don’t really know what it is. I’m having so much fun building it that maybe it’s better to call it my Playground instead of a portfolio.

And who am I?

I’m still figuring that out. I grew up in the small country of Andorra, obsessed with movies and videogames. Growing up in the Pyrenees made me fall in love with the mountains, though I didn’t realize it until I moved to Barcelona.

I’m a person of obsessions: I always find something new to dive into, go down a rabbit hole for two weeks, and then move my attention somewhere else. I don’t know much about anything, but I know a little about a lot, and I’m really happy about that. It allows me to play with different tools, softwares, and ideas until I stumble across something that excites me.

In Barcelona, I started a degree in audiovisual design, and it quickly became my main obsession. There is something very beautiful about design—something romantic, something I fell in love with. I feel that design can genuinely make people feel good, even happy.

And that’s what I want to do.
